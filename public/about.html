About Me

Hi! My name is Ege. I’m 17 years old and I live in Turkey. I’ve always been curious about technology, and over time I found myself especially interested in programming and web development.

I’m still at the very beginning of my journey, learning step by step. Right now, I’m building small projects to practice what I’ve learned so far. It’s exciting to see how a few lines of code can turn into something real on the screen.

For me, programming is more of a hobby than a career plan at the moment. I enjoy experimenting, making mistakes, and figuring out how things work. My main goal is to keep improving and one day turn this passion into something bigger.
